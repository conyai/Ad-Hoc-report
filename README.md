# ☕ Ad-Hoc Revenue Drop Analysis — Coffee Shop

> Exploratory analysis of revenue decline through the lens of new customer acquisition dynamics.

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

The coffee shop experienced periodic revenue drops that weren't immediately explainable by seasonality alone. This ad-hoc report investigates whether a decline in **new customer flow** is a key driver of revenue dips.

---

## 🎯 Goal

Identify the relationship between **new customer acquisition** and **daily/weekly revenue** to help the business understand what's causing revenue drops and where to focus attention.

---

## 🔍 Key Findings

- A drop in new customer flow strongly correlates with revenue decline in specific periods
- Revenue dips are not random — they follow patterns tied to customer acquisition dynamics
- Visualizations reveal clear trends that can inform marketing and operational decisions
